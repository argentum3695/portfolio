2025 UCAT ANZ Statistics

Hover over the graph to see what percentile a score corresponds to!

Data sourced from the UCAT ANZ website at https://www.ucat.edu.au/results/test-statistics/
The 2025 UCAT percentile calculator was used to obtain more detailed data than what the summary statistics offer. These calculators are usually only available for a limited time, which is why I graphed the data.
Scores are provided in increments of 10, as far as I know; smaller increments were only used to acquire data at a higher resolution.
